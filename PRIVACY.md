# Privacy

Kanvaz collects nothing and connects to nothing.

- **No accounts.** There's no sign-up, login, or user identity of any kind.
- **No telemetry or analytics.** Kanvaz does not track usage, errors, or
  any other data.
- **No internet access.** Kanvaz makes no network requests. It works the
  same with your network cable unplugged.
- **No cloud storage.** Your boards are saved as `.kanvaz` files on your
  own computer, wherever you choose to save them. Kanvaz never uploads
  anything anywhere.
- **Your media stays yours.** Images, video, and audio you drop onto the
  canvas are embedded directly into your `.kanvaz` file. Nothing leaves
  your machine unless you manually share that file yourself.

If you ever find anything in the source code that contradicts this,
please open an issue — it would be a bug, not intended behavior.
